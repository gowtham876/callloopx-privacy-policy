Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2026-05-02

CallLoopX ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

  • Phone Numbers: Used only for initiating calls through the app.
  • Call Status Data: Used to manage retry logic and call automation.
  • App Usage Data: Anonymous data to improve performance and stability.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide auto-calling and retry functionality
  • To maintain call history and tracking
  • To improve app performance and user experience
  • To send important notifications related to app usage

3. Data Storage and Security

All user data is stored locally on your device. We do not store or transmit your personal data to our servers.

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties.

5. Permissions We Use

  • CALL_PHONE: To initiate phone calls
  • READ_PHONE_STATE: To detect call status and manage retries
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS: To show call updates and alerts

6. Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party services to improve app functionality:

  • Firebase Crashlytics: For crash reporting and diagnostics
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM): For push notifications

These services may collect limited technical data as per their own privacy policies.

7. Data Retention

All data remains on your device unless you delete the app or clear app data.

8. Your Rights

You have full control over your data. You can delete app data anytime through your device settings.

9. Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
